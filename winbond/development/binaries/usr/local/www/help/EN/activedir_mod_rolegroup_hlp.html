Modifying Role Group - Help

Use this page to modify a role group's domain name and privilege level.

Role Group Name

The name of the role group being configured.

Role Group Domain Name

The domain where the role group is located.
  • - Domain Name is a string of 255 alpha-numeric characters.
  • - Special symbols hyphen, underscore and dot are allowed.

Role Group Privilege

The level of privilege to assign to this role group.


Actions

Modify

Click on Modify button to accept the modifications and return to the Role Group list.

Cancel

Click on cancel this modification and return to the Role Group list.
